The Student Assessment
Management System

A system that would help manage the assessments of students within the Ateneo De Davao - School Of Education. This system would help manage the recording of grades for the teachers, provides the students the ability to view their respective grades and helps manages the creation, printing and distribution of report cards.

Student

Allows the viewing and tracking of student's grades and performance

Teacher

Allows for the efficient recording, tracking and distribution of student grades and assessments

Administrator

An administrator system that allows the efficient overseeing of accounts within the system as well as the printing of the report cards
